Instructions for sharing KeyShotXR

Below is your KeyShotXR. To view the KeyShotXR by itself open the Ful Car_XR.48.html file in the Ful Car_XR.48 folder.

 

Uploading to and displaying on your website:

Upload the 'Ful Car_XR.48' folder with all of its contents to your webserver. Copy (Ctrl-C/Cmd-C) and Paste (Ctrl-V/Cmd-V) the code below to your webpage.

[Code]: 

<div style="max-width: 1024px; max-height: 576px;">

  <div style="left: 0px; width: 100%; height: 0px; position: relative; padding-bottom: 56.25%; overflow: hidden;">

    <iframe src="Ful Car_XR.48.html"

                 allowfullscreen

                 style="position: absolute; top: 0px; left: 0px; height: 100%; width: 1px; min-width: 100%; *width: 100%;"

                 frameborder="0"

                 scrolling="no">

    </iframe>

  </div>

</div>

If you share your KeyShotXR via email, send the entire zipped 'Ful Car_XR.48' folder to the recipient(s).
